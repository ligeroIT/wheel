Privacy Policy for Wheel Decide

Last updated: November 30, 2025

Thank you for using "Wheel Decide" ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App via Facebook Login.

1. Data Controller

The data controller for this App is Ligero IT. If you have any questions, please contact us using the form below.

2. Information We Collect

The App uses Facebook Login for authentication. With your permission, we may access the following public information:

  • Name: To display your name on the wheel or player list.
  • Profile Picture: To display your avatar in the game.
  • Email Address: Used as a unique identifier for your user account.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate you and log you into the App.
  • To facilitate the game mechanics (spinning the wheel, displaying participants).
  • To save the game state in real-time.

4. Data Sharing and Storage

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is stored securely using cloud services (Google Firebase/Render) required for the App's functionality.

5. Data Deletion Instructions

Per Facebook Platform rules, you have the right to request that your data be deleted.

Option A (Self-Service via Facebook):
You can revoke the App's access to your data at any time via your Facebook settings:

  1. Go to your Facebook "Settings & Privacy".
  2. Select "Settings" > "Apps and Websites".
  3. Find "Wheel Decide" in the list and click "Remove".

Option B (Manual Request):
If you wish for us to manually delete any game records associated with your account from our database, please use the contact form below.
